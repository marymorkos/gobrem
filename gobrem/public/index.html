Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025 · BREM Travel Inc.

Your Data Stays on BREM

Unlike other travel platforms that redirect you to third-party sites and share your information across dozens of partners, BREM processes everything in-house. When you book on BREM, your data never touches Expedia, Booking.com, Airbnb, or any other external platform.

What We Collect

  • Name, email, and date of birth for account creation and age verification
  • Travel document information (passport/ID) — encrypted at rest with AES-256
  • Payment information — processed via Stripe, never stored on BREM servers
  • Search and booking history to power smart recommendations
  • Device type and general location for routing and currency display

How We Protect It

  • All data transmitted via TLS 1.3 (256-bit SSL encryption)
  • Travel documents encrypted with AES-256 before storage
  • PCI DSS compliant payment processing — card data never touches our servers
  • Two-factor authentication available on all accounts
  • Regular third-party security audits
  • We never sell your data. Period.

Apple Pay & Google Pay

When you pay via Apple Pay or Google Pay, your actual card number is never shared with BREM. Apple and Google use tokenization — we receive a one-time payment token only. This means even if BREM were ever breached, your card details would be safe.

Your Rights

  • Download all your data at any time from Account Settings
  • Delete your account and all associated data permanently
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
